KITS: Your Pathway to Excellence

KITS (KKR & KSR Institute of Technology & Sciences) is affiliated to JNTU Kakinada and approved by AICTE New Delhi. KITS campus is located in a serene and pollution free atmosphere at Vinjanampadu 5Km from Guntur on Pratipadu Road. The campus spread over 11 acres of scenic landscape is an ideal place for educational pursuits.
