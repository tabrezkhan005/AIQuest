Project Expo: A Platform to Showcase Your Projects

The Prototype Project Showcase is an opportunity for teams of 4-5 members to present innovative, working prototypes in either the software or hardware domain. The prototypes must be functional and accompanied by a detailed explanation of their features, design, and application. Participants are required to bring their own components to complete the project. The event emphasizes creativity, technical expertise, and practical implementation. Winners will be awarded attractive prizes, including ₹5000 with a certificate and memento for the first prize and ₹3000 with a certificate and memento for the second prize. This is the perfect platform to bring ideas to life and gain recognition for technical brilliance.

Innovate and Showcase: Build Your Own Prototype for Project Expo!

Teams of 4–5 members are invited to participate in the Project Expo and demonstrate their creativity and problem-solving skills! Bring your ideas to life by creating your own prototype and showcasing it to the audience.

Collaborate, innovate, and let your project speak for itself. Whether it's a groundbreaking invention or a practical solution, this is your chance to shine and leave a mark on the expo stage!

Team Up for Success: Collaborate and Create with Your Squad!

Gather your brightest minds and form a team of 4–5 members to participate in this thrilling event! Teamwork and collaboration are the keys to unlocking your full potential and achieving greatness together.

Combine your skills, share your ideas, and take on challenges as a united force. The stage is set for innovation and creativity—let’s see what your team can accomplish!

Punctuality Matters: Be at Your Desk on Time!

Teams of 4–5 members, ensure every member is present and ready at their desk on time. Timeliness is key to staying organized and making the most of the event.

Start strong and stay focused as you collaborate, innovate, and bring your ideas to life. Remember, success begins with being prepared and present—let’s make every moment count!

Take Your Time: Present Without Limits!

Teams of 4–5 members are encouraged to give their best without the pressure of the clock. There’s no time limit—focus on delivering a thoughtful and impactful presentation.

Use this freedom to explain your ideas clearly, collaborate effectively, and showcase your creativity to its fullest. The stage is yours—make every moment count and leave a lasting impression!

Present with Impact: Create a PPT & Chart for Your Project!

Teams of 4–5 members are required to present their innovative ideas in both a PowerPoint presentation and a chart for a visually engaging experience.

Work together to organize your thoughts, design your slides, and use charts to effectively communicate your concept. A well-structured presentation paired with clear visuals will make your idea stand out and leave a lasting impression!
