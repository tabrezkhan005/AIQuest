IDEA PITCHING: A Platform to Showcase Your Innovative Ideas

The Idea Pitching event is a unique opportunity for students to showcase their innovative ideas and compete for exciting prizes. This event is designed to foster a spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship among students, and to provide a platform for them to share their ideas with a wider audience. The event will feature a series of pitches from students, followed by a panel discussion and Q&A session . The winner of the event will receive a cash prize and other exciting rewards. So, get ready to pitch your innovative ideas and win big at the Idea Pitching event!
