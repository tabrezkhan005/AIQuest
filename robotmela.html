Robotics Redefined: Join the Fun at Robot Mela!

The "ROBOT MELA" is an exciting and interactive event that brings together students to explore the fascinating world of robotics. Participants engage in the design and construction of innovative robots, showcasing their creativity and technical skills. The event features live demonstrations and competitions, allowing students to present their robotic creations in action. With a focus on collaboration, problem-solving, and hands-on learning, the ROBOT MELA inspires the next generation of engineers and innovators while fostering a spirit of friendly competition and teamwork.
